Fun Fact:

I designed my very first website almost a decade ago. I wanted to sell clothing online and didn't have money to hire a professional designer, and I like to think of myself as a take charge, no job is too hard kind of guy. Of course, now days there are platforms like Wix and Big Cartel, but these didn't exist then. My desire to design a site was need driven at the time, so once it was done, so was my desire to develop websites. Fast forward...After doing some soul searching and careful consideration, I decided to get back into web development in January of 2017. I've been coding, reading, following tutorials, taking online courses and working on projects day and night ever since. Phew!!! It's hard to imagine a day or two passing without me doing something related to web development. In fact, I look forward to a rewarding career in this field!

Thanks for reading. You rock!!!
